legal talk and travel diary

Hi! I am Ibukun a qualified lawyer in Nigeria with experience in resolving complex legal issues. I am an alumnus of the University of Lagos, Akoka, where I obtained a Bachelor of Law degree and I have a Master’s degree from Northwestern University Pritzker School of Law, in the United States.

With nearly a decade of successful practice, I have provided a wide spectrum of advisory, dispute resolution, real estate, corporate & commercial law services, including trademark, copyright, company and employment law and represented clients up to the highest Courts in Nigeria.

I am an advocate for the promotion of human rights and I was an intern at the United Nations in New York. I also have a YouTube channel dedicated to sharing videos that simplify legal issues to everyday people.
